What the Dashboard Shows
1. Profit by State

Uttar Pradesh and Maharashtra contribute the most, with smaller states adding steady but lower profits.

2. Sales and Profit Trends by Month

Clear fluctuations across the year, with stronger months like January and December, and negative dips around mid-year.

3. Category and Sub-category Breakdown

Clothing has the highest quantity share

Printers and bookcases bring the highest profit

4. Customer-Level Insights

Customers like Harivansh and Madhav generate the highest order amounts.

5. Payment Mode Patterns

COD is the most used payment method, followed by UPI and debit card.
